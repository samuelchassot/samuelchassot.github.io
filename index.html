About

My name is Samuel Chassot. I am a PhD candidate in LARA at EPFL, Switzerland, supervised by Prof. Viktor Kunčak, working on Formal Methods and Software Verification.

Before starting my PhD, I obtained a MSc degree in Computer Science from EPFL, Switzerland

Outside of research, I am a watchmaking enthusiast and practice watchmaking in a self taught manner.

Research

Currently my research is focused on software verification. I work mainly with and on the Stainless verifier.

My latest projects include:

  • The verification of the LongMap of the Scala standard library, a mutable hashtable based on open addressing. This work will be published at IJCAR24.
  • Implementing and verifying an implementation of Regex matching with derivatives. This Regex engine is used to implement a lexer (or tokeniser), verified to be correct and invertible.
